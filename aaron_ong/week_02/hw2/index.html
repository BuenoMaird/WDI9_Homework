About Us

Codes In The Clouds, the quintet of Ciaran Morahan, Stephen Peeling, Jack Major and Joe Power hail from Dartford, England – a town not known for much besides giving birth to The Rolling Stones. Since 2007 they have dedicated themselves to producing heartfelt music to rival their peers and firmly place CITC as ‘Ones To Watch’ in a vastly growing instrumental rock scene.
